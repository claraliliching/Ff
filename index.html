My Weight Loss Journey

Achieving Goals, One Step at a Time

Goals

Short-Term Goals: Lose 0.5 kg per week.
Long-Term Goals: Lose 15 kg in total.

Methods

Diet Changes:

I focused on a balanced diet that includes:

  • Increased Fruits and Vegetables: At least five servings daily to feel full while consuming fewer calories.
  • Whole Grains: Switched to brown rice and whole grain bread for more fiber and nutrients.
  • Lean Proteins: Included chicken, fish, beans, and legumes to support muscle maintenance.
  • Portion Control: Measured portions and used smaller plates to avoid overeating.
  • Limiting Processed Foods: Avoided processed snacks and sugary drinks, opting for water or herbal teas.
Exercise Plan:

My exercise routine includes:

  • Cardio Workouts: At least 150 minutes of aerobic activity weekly, like brisk walking or cycling.
  • Strength Training: Focused on all major muscle groups twice a week.
  • Flexibility and Balance: Included yoga and stretching to improve flexibility and prevent injuries.
  • Daily Movement: Stayed active throughout the day by walking during breaks and taking the stairs.
