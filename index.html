My Weight Loss Journey

Achieving Goals, One Step at a Time

Goals

Short-Term Goals: Lose 0.5 kg per week.
Long-Term Goals: Lose 15 kg in total.

Methods

Diet Changes:

I focused on a balanced diet that includes:

  • Increased Fruits and Vegetables: I aim for at least five servings of fruits and vegetables each day. This helps me feel full while consuming fewer calories.
  • Whole Grains: I switched to whole grains, such as brown rice and whole grain bread, which provide more fiber and nutrients.
  • Lean Proteins: I incorporate lean proteins like chicken, fish, beans, and legumes to support muscle maintenance while losing weight.
  • Portion Control: I measure my portions to avoid overeating, using smaller plates and bowls to help control serving sizes.
  • Limiting Processed Foods: I try to avoid processed snacks and sugary drinks, opting instead for water, herbal teas, or homemade snacks.
Exercise Plan:

My exercise routine includes:

  • Cardio Workouts: I engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity each week, such as brisk walking, cycling, or swimming.
  • Strength Training: I do strength training exercises at least twice a week, focusing on all major muscle groups to build lean muscle mass.
  • Flexibility and Balance: I incorporate activities like yoga or stretching to improve flexibility and help prevent injuries.
  • Daily Movement: I make an effort to stay active throughout the day, such as taking the stairs instead of the elevator and walking during breaks.
